
HOW TO PLAY: 15-PUZZLE (HUMAN VS AI)
=====================================

OBJECTIVE
---------
Arrange the tiles in numerical order (1-15) with the empty space at the bottom-right corner.
You are competing against an AI timer!

CONTROLS
--------
- CLICK a tile adjacent to the empty space to move it into the empty space.
- You cannot move diagonal tiles.

GAMEPLAY
--------
1. The game starts with a shuffled board.
2. You have 180 seconds to solve the puzzle.
3. Every move you make earns you 10 points.
4. Solving the puzzle gives a 100-point bonus + time bonus!
5. If time runs out, the AI wins.

POWER-UPS
---------
- UNDO: Made a mistake? Click 'Undo' to revert your last move.
- FREEZE AI: (Not active in this version, but part of the UI)
- RESET: Start a new game with a fresh shuffle.

SCORING
-------
- Try to solve it in as few moves as possible to maximize your efficiency score!

GOOD LUCK!
